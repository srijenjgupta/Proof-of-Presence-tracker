What is the POP Tracker?

This tool helps small businesses verify that their field staff (sales, delivery, technicians) have physically visited a client's location using GPS geofencing.

  • Admins: Register your business, generate Staff IDs, and drop map pins to assign client territories.
  • Staff: Log in with your ID to see your daily roster. You must be within 100 meters of the client's map pin to successfully "Check-In".
