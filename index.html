What is the POP Tracker?

This tool helps small businesses verify field staff visits using GPS geofencing and tracks visit outcomes.

  • Admins: Register your business, assign client territories, and track verified sales data.
  • Staff: Log in to see your roster. You must be within 100 meters of the client to submit your Visit Report and "Check-In".
